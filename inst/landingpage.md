# Welcome to **ReHeaT 2**
> the **Re**ference for the **Hea**rt failure **T**ranscriptome 2


### Overview

ReHeaT is a user-friendly portal for exploring and querying the consensus multicellular program of human end-stage heart failure.

This program integrates data from four single-nucleus studies with 21 bulk transcriptomic studies to identify robust, cell-type-specific gene expression patterns in heart failure.
